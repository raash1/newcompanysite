Our History We started the Hot Topic Foundation in 2004 at the suggestion of our store associates. Over the last ten years, the Foundation gave nearly $9 million in grants to non-profit organizations that create music and arts programs for young people across the country - at schools and beyond. We're proud of what we've accomplished. And we know - with your help - we can enrich many more young lives.
You Can Help We think this stuff really matters. In fact, many Hot Topic employees actively support the foundation's efforts. You can help, too:
Round up your purchases at Hot Topic stores and Hottopic.com to the next dollar - the change will go straight to the Hot Topic Foundation.
Make contributions in our stores and online. Remember - no contribution is too small.
