Hot Topic's backstory

Hot Topic came to the malls in 1988. It opened up as a music inspired accessory store, something that wasn't too common. This store attracted many teens as well as many music fans. They started selling merchandise, attracting even more customers. Now, Hot Topic offers band related apparel and accessories, licensed merchandise, and fashion apparel and accessories. There are more that 600 stores around and customers can shop online as well!
